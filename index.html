Bio

Throughout my life I've had a passion for computers, mathmatics, and logic. Although I was initially hesitant to go into such a demanding and challenging field, I was accepted into and enrolled in the BS in Computer Science program at St. Ambrose University in 2019. Pushing myself to follow my ideal career path paid off, and in the summer of 2020 I completed the National Science Foundation's Research Experiences for Undergraduates program at The University of Iowa. For this project, I spent ten weeks creating a VAE neural network for the Big Five personality test and gained invaluable experience. In the fall of 2021, I was additionally accepted into and enrolled in the MS in Information Technology Management 4+1 program at St. Ambrose University. My overall goal is now to inspire others to gain the confidence to enter into this amazing career field, especially other girls and women.
